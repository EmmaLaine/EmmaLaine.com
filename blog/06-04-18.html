About Me

My name is Emma and I am a marine fish enthusiast! The addiction started when I worked at a large pet store chain back when I was going to college. Our previous fish expert had just quit and I was asked if I wanted to take her position. I wasn't so sure at first, but after a week of being pestered about it by my managers I decided to go for even though I knew next to nothing about fish keeping. Next thing you know I am buying a 55 gallon aquarium and starting a saltwater reef tank. But all of that is a story for another time! Welcome and I hope you enjoy this next fish journey with me!
