North Woods Aquatics

North Woods Aquatics is a mock marine fish store website created based on my interest in the hobby of keeping saltwater reef tanks. As a saltwater enthusiast I visited a lot of fish websites and was disappointed by the lack of design and functionality.

North Woods Aquatics was created to show that even an online fish store can be beautiful and function in a way that aids the user in finding the best fish for their aquarium.

DCF SPARC

While working for the Department of Children and Families I had the opportunity to work with a team to upgrade the SPARC website from a predominantly HTML site to a content management system (CMS).

I was part of a team of three that designed and developed the new site. I produced the new web pages based on content from the old site that our client indicated was still valid. I also contributed to the color scheme. Joe Harrison created the Drupal template/theme and setup the modules. Phil Staley handled the search functionality and project management while aiding both Joe and me in any area necessary.

The main goal of the project was to have a simple design that allowed external users to find and access reports and documentation with ease.

  • CMS - Drupal
  • HTML
  • CSS

Ramen Re-design

I was tasked with re-designing Maruchan Ramen packaging for a school project. My instructor acted as the client. I made a few sketched drafts of potential designs we could develop and she whittled down what she liked and what she didn't, sometimes being quite vague as a client might be.

The final design was chosen to target a youth demographic that is interested in the anime genre. The design can be used across all the Ramen flavors while a simple change of the packaging color would help indicate the flavor visually.
